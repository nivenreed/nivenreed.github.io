About Me.

I am a trainee Web Developer, based in South East London. This site is a place for me to demonstrate my journey as I learn to code.
I will display the projects that I work on and demonstrate the skills I have learnt.


After spending the last 17 years working in sales, I now feel it is time for a new challenge.
I think my sales career has given me the skills to be able to communicate well with not only a team but also the client.

Interests and Hobbies

When not learning to code I enjoy the outdoors and my hobbies include photography, bee keeping and bbqing on my ceramic Kamado grill.

Bee Keeping.

I have had an interest in bees from a very young age. About 8 years ago I decided to learn more. After attending a beginner beekeeping course I got my firts colony and have been keeping bees ever since.

Bbq.

During lock down I decided to invest in a bbq I had been after for many years! The Kamado is a ceramic bbq that you can cook anything you would normally cook in the oven but outside over coal. This quickly turned into a new hobby or should I say passion. There really is nothing better than cooking outside.

Photography.

Photography is another hobby I have had for many years. My very very first Job when leaving college was working in a photographic shop. I learn't so much from how to operate any camera to printing and developing films. There were no digital cameras back when I started! Whenever I can I love to take one of my many cameras out and get creative. Even sometimes dig out one of the old film cameras.
